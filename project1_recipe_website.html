Rava Uttapam

Rava uttapam is a savory pancake made with semolina, yogurt, and veggies, that can be enjoyed with chutney or sambhar.

Ingredients:

Curd | Water | Salt | Oil or ghee | Onion | Tomato | Carrot | Capsicum | Green chili | Ginge | Coriander leaves | Curry leaves | Mustard seeds (optional) | Baking soda (optional)

Bhel

Bhel is a crunchy and tangy snack made with puffed rice, sev, and chutneys, that can be customized with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients:

Puffed rice | Sev | Onion | Tomato | Green chili | Raw mango | Coriander leaves | Peanuts | Chaat masala | Lemon juice | Salt | Sweet tamarind chutney | Spicy green chutney | Garlic chutney (optional)
